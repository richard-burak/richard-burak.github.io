Finally, my 30 second script for the video to be filmed on Tuesday December 13th has been completed:

Hi, my name is Richard Burak, and I was among those responsible for building the Solar Panel Project. The project is built using a Broadcom development platform to interface with a solar cell and a variety of sensors. All of the data is gathered using these on-board sensors, which collect information about the humidity, temperature, barometric pressure, and light level around the hardware. Because the power generation of the solar cell is also tracked, it can be correlated with the weather data and used to further our research into solar energy.
